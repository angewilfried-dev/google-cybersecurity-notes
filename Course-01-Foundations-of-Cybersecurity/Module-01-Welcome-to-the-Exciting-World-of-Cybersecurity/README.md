## Key Takeaways

- Cybersecurity helps protect systems, networks, and sensitive information.
- Entry-level cybersecurity analysts help monitor and defend organizational assets.
- Both technical and soft skills are essential for a successful cybersecurity career.
- Phishing attacks often use urgency, fake domains, suspicious links, and generic greetings to trick users.
